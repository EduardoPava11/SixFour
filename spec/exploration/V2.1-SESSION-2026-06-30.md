## Headline: the instrument works, verified on real hardware

A real capture was AirDropped and parsed (`sixfour_48bc23e0`). Findings:

- `field_source: camera_box` in the manifest: the LIVE Metal soft-splat kernel ran on the device, not
  the fallback proxy. This is the on-device confirmation that could not be gotten headless.
- The `v21_ingest` validator passed: manifest + shapes + the contested cross-check (the device's mode
  margin is byte-identical to the trainer's re-derivation, so device and trainer cannot silently drift).
- Soft-splat confirmed: every bin has exactly 123904 mass, 100% divisible by 16. That decodes as
  `11*11 box * 64 frames * W=16` sub-level budget. The discarded 10-bit sub-LSB bits really are in the
  field (deposited as the mass split across adjacent levels).
- This capture was high-motion (handheld, 3.2 s): mean 152 of 256 levels nonzero per bin, per-bin
  entropy 4.15 nats out of a 5.55 max (75%), only 32% of mass within +/-3 of the peak. The probability
  functions are genuinely broad and multi-modal, the V2.1 thesis made concrete.
- The GIF mode is a weak collapse here: mean margin 2.5%, zero confident bins. The field carries far
  more than the byte, which is exactly why the model trains on the field, not the GIF.
- The per-channel argmax collapse is speckly (false colour where R, G, B peak at different levels). The
  shipped GIF looks cleaner because it uses the k-means joint OKLab palette. That gap is the argument
  for the learned head: a smart joint collapse should beat per-channel argmax.

CORPUS NOTE: this bundle is near-max-entropy everywhere (global camera shake). A useful corpus needs
variety: braced/static captures (low-entropy, confident modes) as well as motion, so the model learns
to distinguish confident from contested bins rather than "everything is uncertain".

### Probability-field advances (spec-first, byte-exact, cross-language golden)

1. SOFT-SPLAT sub-LSB construction (`accumulateHistSoft` / `s4_v21_accumulate_hist_soft` /
   `v21AccumulateHistSoftKernel`): a sample deposits integer mass W split across the two adjacent value
   levels, so the mass-weighted mean equals the full-precision 10-bit value exactly
   (`lawSoftSplatCentroidExact`). The discarded bits survive as the histogram's first moment. Metal soft
   PSO is preferred over the hard one, nil-safe. The field is a separate pipeline from the shipped GIF,
   so this is zero-risk to the user-visible byte.
2. GAUGE-SAFE palette delta (`paletteDelta` / `s4_v21_palette_delta`): the temporal metric weight,
   permutation-invariant L1 between per-channel value histograms.
3. WASSERSTEIN-1 metric (`paletteW1` / `s4_v21_wdist1d`): true 1-D W1 = L1 of the per-channel value
   CDFs (one integer prefix-sum before the abs-sum). Charges the ground distance mass must travel
   (`lawWDistChargesDistance`: a 1-level drift costs 1, a far jump costs the full span, where total
   variation ties them). Discharges the deferred EMD metric on the byte-exact path, no float, no
   Sinkhorn.
